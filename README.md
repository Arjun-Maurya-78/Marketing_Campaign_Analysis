## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a **multi-page Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard** built in **Power BI**, covering **200 campaigns** across **5 marketing channels** (Social Media Ads, Email Marketing, Google Paid Search, Influencer Partnerships, Affiliate Network), **6 flagship campaign types**, and **5 target audience segments**, tracked monthly across all four quarters.

The dashboard enables marketing teams and leadership to explore campaign performance interactively, with filters for **Campaign Name**, **Marketing Channel**, **Target Segment**, and **Quarter (Q1–Q4)**. It surfaces critical patterns around channel efficiency, budget allocation, seasonal revenue trends, and audience profitability in a single, self-service report.

## 📝 Final Conclusion

The campaign portfolio is profitable overall (25.32x average ROI across all 200 campaigns), but performance is **unevenly distributed across channels, campaigns, and audience segments**. The biggest opportunity is not spending more — it's **reallocating existing budget** toward what is already proven to work: Email Marketing, Budget Shoppers, and the Q3/September window.

**Priority actions to improve marketing ROI:**
- Shift budget from Social Media Ads toward Email Marketing and Affiliate Network, where ROI and CPL are both significantly better.
- Audit and revise the Customer Retention Campaign strategy — low ROI from a warm audience is an efficiency problem, not just a creative one.
- Increase spend and creative investment behind Tech Enthusiasts, the highest-converting but most underfunded segment.
- Rebalance Q4 campaign timing — investigate why December underperforms despite high activity, and consider front-loading budget earlier in the quarter.

This dashboard is designed to enable precise, targeted budget and campaign decisions — every visual is filterable by Campaign, Marketing Channel, Target Segment, and Quarter, ensuring marketing teams act on facts rather than assumptions.
